Phone authentication on iOS (reCAPTCHA / Safari) requires a custom URL scheme registered in Info.plist.

1. Encoded App ID scheme (already added in Info.plist)
   Value: "app-" + GOOGLE_APP_ID from GoogleService-Info.plist with ":" replaced by "-".
   Example for GOOGLE_APP_ID 1:109162143836:ios:14cb6dae4910e47bb8c699:
   app-1-109162143836-ios-14cb6dae4910e47bb8c699

2. If you use a full GoogleService-Info.plist from Firebase Console that includes CLIENT_ID:
   Also add a second URL scheme = REVERSED_CLIENT_ID from that same plist.
   (Firebase prefers this scheme when both are registered.)

3. After changing GOOGLE_APP_ID or bundle ID, update the URL scheme accordingly.

4. In Firebase Console, enable Phone sign-in (and optionally Google) under Authentication.

Reference: https://firebase.google.com/docs/auth/ios/phone-auth#set-up-recaptcha-verification
